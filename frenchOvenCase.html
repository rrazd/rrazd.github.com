The French Oven UI & UX Responsive Ecommerce Website

Background

The French Oven is a bakery that sells frozen baked goods. They specialize in both custom orders and large scale production. They are known for their colorful sweet frosting and whimsical yet professional designs. The bakery wanted to increase their custom order business and explore ecommerce. They wanted a visualization of what a modern, elegant ecommerce website would look like.

Competitive Analysis and Research

Competitive Analysis was performed to assess other successful ecommerce bakery site's information architecture. One that really stood out was Mahzedahr Bakery - for its usage of large high quality product images, intuitive navigation, well placed cart icon and simple checkout process. Examining other award winning ecommerce websites and reading case studies highlighted the importance of key factors such as high-resolution zoomable photos.

The client was asked to provide information about their customer demographics. Moreover, their existing website's analytics data was examined.

User Stories and User Flows

User stories were used to define requirements and ensured that the user was always at the center of any decisions during the design and development process:

  • As a user, I want to browse products

  • As a user, I want to view details about a specific product

  • As a user, I want to view ratings and reviews for a product

  • As a user, I want to add a product to my cart

  • As a user, I want to pay for my items with a credit card, Amazon Payments or PayPal

  • As a user, I want to sign up for an account
