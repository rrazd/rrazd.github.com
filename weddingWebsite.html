Development, Responsive Webdesign and UI

Background

The client wanted a wedding website to be designed and developed from scratch in vanilla javascript, HTML and CSS. All design and UI elements, including the logo needed to be original. The wedding was a traditional indian wedding and so the color pallette chosen had to reflect the vibrance of an indian wedding.

Research

Research was conducted to see how other wedding websites were organized, including the main sections that were present and the different ways information was presented.

Development

Development was done by myself and another developer. We worked collaboratively using GIT to power our development workflow. Particular attention was given to responsive design to ensure that the website was fully mobile compatible in both potrait and landscape orientation. Conclusion

The final product given to the client was complete wireframes and designs of an iOS mobile application that allows users to find food trucks near them, view a menu, select items for purchase, checkout and review previous purchases.
